Calby Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what Calby collects, how it is used, and what choices are available to you when you use the app.

Support Privacy Choices

What Calby does

Calby helps users log food, analyze meal and fridge photos, track nutrition progress, and maintain a wellness profile tied to an account.

Calby is a wellness and nutrition product. It is not medical care, a medical device, or a substitute for professional medical advice.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and account identifiers from Supabase Auth.
  • Profile information: name, age, height, weight, unit preference, goal, diet preference, allergies or intolerances, activity level, skin concern, and reminder preferences that the user chooses to enter.
  • App progress data: saved meals, nutrition summaries, scan history, reminder setup, and related app state that syncs to the account.
  • Scan inputs: food and fridge images submitted for analysis, plus structured scan context such as goal or portion hints.
  • Optional meal details: short food-scan notes such as dish name, ingredients, rough weight, or sauce and oil context.
  • Usage and security data: request timestamps, approximate request size, status codes, token counts, and limited network metadata such as IP address for abuse prevention and service protection.
  • Barcode lookups: barcode values scanned or typed when looking up packaged foods.

How we use information

  • To create and maintain accounts.
  • To save and restore profile data and synced app progress across devices.
  • To analyze food and fridge photos and return nutrition-related results.
  • To operate reminders and account settings users choose to enable.
  • To detect abuse, rate-limit requests, measure usage, and control service costs.
  • To troubleshoot issues, support users, and improve the app experience.

How Calby processes images

When a live food or fridge scan is run, the image is sent to Calby's backend so the request can be authenticated, validated, rate-limited, and processed for analysis.

The backend stores usage and security metadata about the request. Signed-in body progress photos are backed up to a private Supabase Storage bucket so they can restore on another iPhone.

Third-party services

  • Supabase: authentication, synced profile data, synced app state, storage, and backend functions.
  • AI processing providers: used by Calby's backend to analyze food and fridge images that users intentionally submit for AI results.
  • Open Food Facts: barcode-based packaged food lookups.

Your choices

  • Profile details and preferences can be updated in the app.
  • Reset my data clears synced profile and app-state data for the signed-in account and removes related local data from the current iPhone.
  • Delete account permanently deletes the Calby account, synced data, and local files tied to that account on the current iPhone.
  • Users can contact Calby about account deletion, privacy requests, or support follow-up through the public support page or support email.

Email calby.support@gmail.com for support and privacy requests.

Children's privacy

Calby is not intended for children under 13, and Calby does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
