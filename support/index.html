What support can help with

Account and sign-in

Magic link issues, Apple or Google sign-in problems, password reset questions, and account recovery.

Sync and restore

Restoring profile data, synced app state, and backed-up body progress photos after moving to a new iPhone.

Scans and app behavior

Meal scan failures, fridge scan issues, temporary rate-limit blocks, and unexpected AI results.

Common help topics

  • Sign-in issues: confirm you are using the same sign-in method you used previously.
  • Changed phones: sign in with the same Calby account to restore synced profile data and app state.
  • Resetting progress: use Reset my data in Profile to clear synced profile and app-state data for the signed-in account and remove related local data on the current iPhone.
  • Deleting the account: use Delete account in Profile if you want the entire Calby account permanently removed.
  • Missing photos: body progress photos back up to the signed-in account. Stay signed in and give restore a moment to finish.
  • Scan limits: Calby applies backend usage protections and may temporarily block scans if requests happen too quickly.

Privacy and account requests

For privacy-related questions, account deletion follow-up, or data handling concerns, email calby.support@gmail.com and mention that the request is privacy-related.
